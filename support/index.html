Frequently Asked Questions

How does voice input work?

Tap the microphone button and speak naturally. SaySo uses AI to listen to what you say and converts your words into structured tasks in real time. You can describe multiple tasks, add details, and SaySo will organize them for you.

How does web import work?

Share a link to SaySo from Safari or any app using the iOS share sheet. SaySo will read the page content and automatically extract actionable steps as tasks. This works great with articles, recipes, tutorials, and more.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your tasks are stored locally on your device by default. If you sign in, your data syncs securely to the cloud, but it is never sold or shared with third parties. We do not use any tracking or analytics. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Do I need an account?

No. SaySo works fully offline without an account. Creating an account is optional and enables cloud sync so your tasks are available across devices.

How do I report a bug?

Send an email to 13olberdingtm@gmail.com with a description of the issue, what you expected to happen, and your device and iOS version. Screenshots are always helpful.
